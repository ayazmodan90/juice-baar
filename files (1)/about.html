Our Manifesto

We Believe Food is Medicine

We started ZEST because we believed that real nutrition shouldn't be complicated.

In a world of processed food and artificial ingredients, we chose the radical act of simplicity — taking the best organic produce from local farms and pressing it into something extraordinary.

Every juice we make is a promise. A promise that what goes into your body is clean, honest, and alive with nutrients.

01
Our Sourcing

Farm to Glass

We partner with 12 local organic farms within 100 miles of each location. Every ingredient is harvested within 24 hours of your juice being pressed. No warehouses. No middlemen. Just the freshest produce going straight from earth to your hands.

02
Our Process

Cold-Pressed Science

Our hydraulic cold-press machines use thousands of pounds of pressure to extract juice without heat. This preserves up to 3× more vitamins, minerals, and enzymes than centrifugal juicing — giving you a living beverage that truly nourishes.

Our Journey

2018

The First Stall

Founder Maya Torres starts selling cold-pressed juices at the Riverside Farmers' Market with a single juicer and a dream.

2019

First Brick & Mortar

ZEST opens its first juice bar in Brooklyn. Sells out every day for the first two months.

2021

Custom Blends Launch

We introduce the Build-Your-Blend system, letting customers create personalized juices with booster add-ins.

2023

National Recognition

Named "Best Juice Bar in America" by Wellness Magazine. Expansion to 4 new cities.

2026

Zero Waste Pledge

All 6 locations now operate zero-waste. Pulp goes to composting, bottles are 100% biodegradable.
